Below you can see our first draft for the wire coil. It was made using carboard, a cleaned out snus container and some tape. This was made to get an idea of what we were working towards before we started production of more refined version. We did some testing on this and it was clear that we needed to make a more precise version with proper measurements.

This was our first attempt at 3D-moddeling a wire coil. The result didnt quite go as planned. The sizes were off and we made some mistakes on this one.

Below you can see the 3D drawings Adrian made for our final physical prototype. This was used as models for 3D-printing our wire coil.
